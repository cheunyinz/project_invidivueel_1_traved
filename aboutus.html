About us

TRAVED. is a student run platform for international students studying in Amsterdam to make the most of their stay in the Netherlands!

TRAVED. is part of the NOTED. International Student Guide Amsterdam.

On this platform you can join or create different trips. Go on a studytrip to Arnhem to learn about the Second World War or go to Giethoorn, know as the Dutch Venice.

Start joining a trip, or create one and explore the Netherlands and beyond!
